# Splyt Brand Color Palette

## Primary Brand Color

- **Main**: `#34D399` – Primary green, used for CTAs, highlights, and key interactions
- **Light**: `#6EE7B7` – Lighter tint for hover states
- **Lighter**: `#A7F3D0` – Even lighter for soft backgrounds
- **Lightest**: `#DBEAFE` – Very light for subtle overlays
- **Dark**: `#14B8A6` – Darker shade for depth and hierarchy
- **Darker**: `#0F766E` – Darkest variant for strong contrast

## Backgrounds

- **Darkest**: `#08131A` – Main background color, very dark navy
- **Darker**: `#071219` – Slightly lighter dark navy
- **Dark**: `#0F172A` – Medium-dark navy
- **Muted**: `#102B26` – Dark with green tint
- **Secondary**: `#102A25` – Alternative dark green background

## Surface & Content

- **Light**: `#EAF7F0` – Light cream for logo backgrounds and content areas
- **Lighter**: `#DDFBF4` – Very light green for accents
- **Foreground**: `#F7F5EF` – Off-white for text and light surfaces

## Text

- **Foreground**: `#F7F5EF` – Primary text color on dark backgrounds
- **Muted**: `#A7B3AF` – Secondary/muted text for reduced prominence
- **Emphasis**: `#E5F3FF` – Special emphasis text

## Accent

- **Main**: `#14B8A6` – Teal accent for secondary highlights
- **Soft**: `#CCFBF1` – Light teal for soft backgrounds and overlays

## Status Colors

- **Success**: `#10B981` – Green for successful actions/states
- **Warning**: `#F59E0B` – Amber for warnings and caution
- **Danger**: `#EF4444` – Red for errors and critical states

## Gradients

### Logo Mark Gradient
```
linear-gradient(135deg, #2D6A4F, #0F172A)
```
Used in the primary logo mark for depth and visual interest.

### Accent Gradient
```
linear-gradient(135deg, #34D399, #14B8A6)
```
Secondary gradient from primary green to teal accent, used for accents and hover states.

## Usage Guide

- **Primary Green (#34D399)**: Use for all primary CTAs, buttons, and key interactions
- **Dark Backgrounds (#08131A)**: Use as the main page background
- **Light Surface (#EAF7F0)**: Use for logo backgrounds and light content areas
- **Text Foreground (#F7F5EF)**: Use for all primary text on dark backgrounds
- **Muted Text (#A7B3AF)**: Use for secondary, helper, or less important text

## Accessibility

All color combinations maintain WCAG AA contrast ratios for accessibility:
- Primary text on dark backgrounds: 15:1+ contrast
- Muted text on dark backgrounds: 7:1+ contrast
- Primary green on light backgrounds: 5:1+ contrast
